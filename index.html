About Me

I'm a simple, passionate person with love for artistic designs and fashion. I also like to travel, meet people, listen to music, reading, working out, etc. Growing up i always liked to design things but i ended up in science school. While there, i was struggling to connect with any of the courses, at a point i was asking myself, "what am i doing here?".

I found this passion for Tech in college where i majored in computer science. Initially, i had interest in computer networking, went to cisco networking school after college. I got a job after the program as a systems and network administrator. I've worked in different fields - electronic engineering, project managements, etc as a Tech support. Currently, i work for Medtronic in Danvers, MA. as a Data Entry Analyst.

Recently, i did a cybersecurity program at University of Maryland and was part of a project that involved programming the web. It got more interesting as the program went on. After the program, i got motivated and enthusiastic about web programming and enrolled in a full stack web programming course at Harvard Extension School.
